Teen-Speak for Dumbies

Theres a multitude of different things teens say on the daily that boggles the mind of parents; well let me help you out and decode the top 5 secert teen lingos

  1. Swipe right: Derived from the user interface of the popular hook-up app Tinder, "swipe right" is a term of approval.
  2. V: Short for "very," as in "I type v fast." So fast that the e, r, and y just get in the way.
  3. Ship: As a noun, ship is short for a romantic relationship. As a verb, it means wanting a romantic relationship. But it's spoken by teens (who have a hard time grappling with their own awkward feelings)
  4. SMH: An acronym for "shake my head" it’s what teens will say about you if you use their slang. For instance; "Woman just said my outfit was on fleek. Smh."
  5. RT: Retweet. Similar to forwarding an email, Twitter lets you echo other people's tweets for your own followers to read.
